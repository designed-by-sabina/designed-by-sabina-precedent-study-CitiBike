visual language analysis_ The CitiBike Rebalancing Study primarily consists of computational diagrams. In it,
you can find heatmaps that show various degrees of concentration of Citi Bikes and
their movement. Heatmaps emphasize the flow of data and highlight locations that
attract riders. To simplify data search, the author provides matrices as an instrument
to track the number of bikes in each location at different times. Both matrices and
heatmaps are created in dark mode data visualization and glowing data aesthetics, rooted
in scientific visualization. The researchers used ArcGIS, Adobe Illustrator, and
Processing to create high-contrast, system-oriented visuals.
